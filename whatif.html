What If Scenarios

What if Eren Didn’t Kill 80% of Humanity?

Imagine a world where Eren Yeager chose a different path—one without the Rumbling. Instead of wiping out 80% of humanity, what if he had found another way to secure freedom for Paradis Island?

1. No Global Genocide

The biggest consequence: millions of lives would be spared. The world wouldn't see Eren as a mass murderer, and Paradis might have had a chance at diplomacy.

2. The Alliance Might Not Exist

If Eren didn’t go extreme, Armin, Mikasa, and the others might not have needed to fight him. The team could have stayed united until the end.

3. Paradise’s Fate

Without the Rumbling, Paradis might still be targeted by the world powers. But maybe, just maybe, there was another way—through peace or strategic negotiation.

4. Eren's Legacy

Eren wouldn't be remembered as a villain or a hero who sacrificed everything. His legacy would’ve been complex, but perhaps less tragic.

What If Naruto Never Chased Sasuke?

Imagine a world where Naruto never chased after Sasuke. Would Sasuke have stayed with Orochimaru forever? Or would Naruto have become Hokage sooner? Fans love exploring these alternate realities and scenarios!
